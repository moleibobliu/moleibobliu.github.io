Molei Liu

Welcome to my personal page!

I am a 4th year Ph.D. student from Department of Biostatistics, Harvard Chan School of Public Health, advised by Tianxi Cai. My thesis advisor committee also includes Lucas Janson and Junwei Lu.

My research interests about statistics include high dimensional statistics, distributed learning, semi-supervised learning, semi-parametric theory and model-X. Besides these modern statistics topics, I also work on electronic health record (EHR) data analysis.

Prior to PhD, I did my undergraduate in the School of Math, Peking University and did my summar intern at Hongyu Zhao's group, Department of Biostatistics, Yale school of Public Health.
